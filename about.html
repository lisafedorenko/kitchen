Corporate Kitchen

The story

It is hard to fit affordable healthy eating into a busy lifestyle. Whether you're working long hours in the city, running your own business, studying or raising a family - cooking can be arduous and time-consuming, going out isn't always an option and it is hard to find take-away that is consistently cheap, healthy and tasty. Even grabbing take-away will likely take 30 mins out of your schedule - assuming a 10 min purchase time and 10 min walk to your favourite vendour.

For me - the needle really dropped when I was working in the city over the Xmas period when most of my go-to-take-away options shut-down for the break. I decided it was time to find an alternative.

It all started with a salad. That was easy enough to make in a micro kitchen. :)

But then things escalated.... after all you can't make friends with salad!!

So I started experimenting... could I make steak? Fish? Lamb? Why not?! Turns out this is reasonably novel - so I wanted to share how I make the meals as an alternative option to:

  1. Cheap junk food nearby
  2. Expensive healthy food nearby
  3. Being organised enough to pack your lunch ahead of time - which /I certainly am not =P

About my work kitchen

My work kitchen is reasonably small - and quite representative of what most small businesses or corporates may have themselves. It contains:

  1. A benchtop
  2. A fridge
  3. A cupboard
  4. A microwave
  5. A sandwich press
  6. A toaster
  7. Plates/ bowls
  8. Knives/ forks - including one sharp knife

The recipes

Timing

Most of the recipes take sub 10 minutes. In general the bottleneck for timing tends to be the choice of carb. Salads are the fastests - only requiring a few minutes to assemble. Brown rice less than a min, cous cous takes 3, potatoes 8, quinoa 10.

Nutrition

I try and eat healthy - this is quite hard and vague - there are so many different tips on how to eat well and in general I find it quite confusing. So the rule I've tried to stick to with these recipes is: 1 x serve of carb 1-2 x serve of protein 1+ serve of greens/ vegetables 1+ something a little tasty
